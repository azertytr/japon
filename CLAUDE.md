## Important Notes

### Temperature & Heat
- July = very hot & humid (30-35°C)
- **Must:** Drink 2-3L water daily + electrolyte drinks
- **Strategy:** Sports in morning, rest 2-5 PM, walk in evening
- **AC breaks:** Plan 1-2 hours in air-conditioned spaces daily

### Hotels by City
- **Tokyo (2+1 nights):** Shinjuku or Shibuya area
- **Osaka (3 nights):** Namba area  
- **Kyoto (6 nights):** Kawaramachi area

### Packing Essentials
- SPF50+ sunscreen (big bottle!)
- Reusable water bottle
- Light, breathable clothing
- Comfortable walking shoes
